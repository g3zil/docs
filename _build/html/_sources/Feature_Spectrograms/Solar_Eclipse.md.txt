**Feature Description**

![CHU 7.85MHz 8April2024](/_static/images/image005.png)

Solar Eclipse - S-curve’s negative Doppler shifts show the ionosphere’s reflection layer receding upwards when the Moon covers the Sun, then the positive section indicates the return of the ionizing sunlight as the Moon leaves the face of the Sun.
